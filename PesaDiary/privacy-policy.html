2. What the App does

PesaDiary helps you understand your M-Pesa activity by reading M-Pesa confirmation SMS messages stored in your device's SMS inbox, parsing transaction details (such as amounts, dates, counterparties, and fees), and presenting summaries and insights inside the App.

3. Information we access and process

The App requests permission to read SMS messages on your device. We use this permission only to access messages that appear to come from M-Pesa (for example, messages from senders whose address contains "MPESA").

From those messages, the App may process:

  • Message date and time
  • Sender address (e.g., MPESA)
  • Message body text (transaction confirmations)
  • Derived transaction details, such as amount, direction (sent/received), counterparty or merchant name, transaction reference code, and transaction cost

The App does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, phone calls, or other SMS senders unless future versions explicitly request additional permissions and update this policy.

4. How we use your information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Display M-Pesa transaction history and analytics within the App
  • Generate monthly summaries, totals, and entity-based breakdowns
  • Filter and organize transactions by date range or category
  • Support optional on-device export of message data to your device's Documents folder, if you enable that feature when available

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

5. Where processing happens

All reading, parsing, and analysis occurs locally on your device. The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not send your SMS content or transaction data to our servers.

6. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs that collect your SMS or financial data.

We may disclose information only if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, users, or the public where permitted by law.

7. Data storage and retention

  • On your device: SMS messages remain in your device's SMS inbox as managed by your phone and messaging system. The App processes them in memory while you use it and does not maintain a separate cloud database of your messages.
  • App state: Certain UI preferences (such as selected date ranges or filters) may be retained locally on your device between sessions using standard Android app state mechanisms.
  • Exports: If you use export functionality, a copy of selected message content may be saved to your device's Documents folder under your control.
  • Android backup: Depending on your device settings, Android may include limited app data in device backup or transfer features. We do not intentionally store your SMS content in app-specific persistent storage for backup.

When you revoke SMS permission or uninstall the App, the App stops accessing your messages. Uninstalling the App does not delete your original SMS messages from your device.

8. Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on your location, our legal bases for processing may include:

  • Your consent — you grant SMS read permission through Android's permission system
  • Legitimate interests — providing the personal finance insights you request from your own device-local data

You may withdraw consent at any time by revoking the SMS permission in your device settings.

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Kenya, or other regions with data protection laws, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability) regarding personal data we process. Because we do not collect data on our servers, many requests can be fulfilled directly by you through device permissions and uninstallation. Contact us if you need assistance.
