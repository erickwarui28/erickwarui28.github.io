How the App Works

Get useful summaries from your existing M-Pesa messages in a few simple steps. No account setup required.

1

Install & Open

Download PesaDiary from Google Play and launch the app on your Android phone.

2

Grant SMS Access

Allow SMS permission so the app can read your M-Pesa confirmation messages.

3

Automatic Extraction

The app detects relevant messages and extracts transaction details automatically.

4

Explore Insights

Use filters and date ranges to analyze trends, entities, and transaction costs.
